## 🎬 Animation Enhancements

### New Animation Library
- `fadeIn` — Fade with upward slide (0.8s)
- `fadeInDown` — Fade from top (0.8s)
- `fadeInUp` — Fade from bottom (0.6s)
- `slideInLeft` — Slide from left (0.8s)
- `slideInRight` — Slide from right (0.8s)
- `glow` — Pulsing glow effect
- `float` — Subtle floating motion

### Hover Interactions
- Buttons lift 2-3px with expanding shadow
- Cards lift 4px with glowing accent shadow
- Text transitions smoothly to accent color
- Links get animated underlines
- Icons scale and animate on interaction

### Scroll-Triggered Animations
- Elements fade in as they enter viewport
- Observer detects 10% visibility threshold
- Smooth cubic-bezier easing (0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- No distracting parallax, just purposeful motion
